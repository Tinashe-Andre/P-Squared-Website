Here's a little more info on us

Founded in 2024, P Squared Cleaning Services is dedicated to providing top-quality cleaning for homes and businesses. Our mission is to deliver a spotless environment with a personal touch, using eco-friendly products and a reliable team you can trust.

At P² we understand the difficulty of maintaining a clean home, especially when you have other things that require your time and energy. We will ensure that we treat your home how we would treat our own. We strive to ensure quality service that will leave every room of your home spotless and most importantly, to your satisfaction.
